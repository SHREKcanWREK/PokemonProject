Well, here are some information about Regions in Pokemon.

In Pokemon, regions are areas of land in the Pokemon universe. Each region has 8 professors and 4 elites. In the main series, there are 8 generations which each have their own region. The 8 Main Regions:
  • Kanto
  • Johto
  • Hoenn
  • Sinnoh
  • Unova
  • Kalos
  • Alola
  • Galar
