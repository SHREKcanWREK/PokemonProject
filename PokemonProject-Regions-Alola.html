Alola is based off Hawaii, USA.

The Region Alola appears in Pokemon Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon.

Pro Tips for Playing on Alola

So you can improve your playing method....

  1. Tap as fast as you can! On Alola, fast attack is very useful.
  2. Don't use Protect shields straight away! Alola is good for attack.
  3. Think twice about switching twice on your pokemon.
  4. First thing you wanna do when you meet a NPC: Upgrade your bike.
  5. Spend some watts to the person called Honey.
  6. Raids. Raids are pretty useful here.
