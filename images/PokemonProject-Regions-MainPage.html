What are Regions?

So.... what are regions?

Pokemon Regions are areas of land in the Pokemon universe. Each region has their own Pokemon Professor, 8 gym leaders and 4 elites. In the main series there are 8 generation games which each have their own regions. The 8 Main Regions are:
  1. Kanto
  2. Johto
  3. Hoenn
  4. Sinnoh
  5. Unova
  6. Kalos
  7. Alola
  8. Galar
